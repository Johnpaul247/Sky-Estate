ABOUT SKY ESTATE

At Sky Estate, we are committed to making your real estate journey smooth and successful. With over 20 years of experience in the local market, we specialize in helping clients buy, sell, and manage properties with personalized service tailored to your needs.

Founded in 2005 by JAYPEE, Sky Estate began with a single office in Owerri, dedicated to offering personalized service and a deep understanding of the local real estate market. Today, we have grown to a team of 50 agents across three offices, helping clients all over country.
